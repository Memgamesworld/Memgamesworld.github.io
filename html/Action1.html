Action game

An action game is a type of video game that focuses on physical challenges
and requires the player to have eye-hand coordination as well as speed of response.
This genre includes various sub-genres, such as fighting games, shooter games,
shooter games, and platform games. Sometimes MOBAs
and real-time strategy are categorized as action games.
